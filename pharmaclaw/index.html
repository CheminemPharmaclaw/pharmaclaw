Pyrimido[4,5-b]indole covalent EGFR inhibitor

🫁

Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC) • EGFR T790M/C797S

Next-generation covalent TKI targeting acquired resistance mutations

PCL-AI-005 features a pyrimido[4,5-b]indole core (scaffold-hopped from classical quinazoline EGFR inhibitors such as erlotinib, gefitinib, and afatinib) linked to an acrylamide warhead for irreversible covalent engagement of Cys797 in the EGFR kinase domain. This design confers high selectivity for the T790M gatekeeper mutant while minimizing wild-type EGFR inhibition.
